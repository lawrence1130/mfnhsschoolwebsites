𝗦𝗖𝗛𝗢𝗢𝗟 𝗡𝗘𝗪𝗦 | 𝗥𝗗 𝗕𝗮𝘆𝗼𝗰𝗼𝘁 𝘃𝗶𝘀𝗶𝘁𝘀 𝗠𝗙𝗡𝗛𝗦, 𝘂𝗽𝗹𝗶𝗳𝘁𝗶𝗻𝗴 𝘁𝗿𝘂𝗲 ‘𝘀𝗽𝗼𝘁𝗹𝗲𝘀𝘀 𝘀𝗽𝗶𝗿𝗶𝘁’

DepEd X Regional Director and Carrier Executive Service Officer III, Dr. Arturo B. Bayocot, made a special visit at Manolo Fortich National High School, encouraging students to reach their dreams and to strengthen their own abilities. In his address during the flag raising ceremony on February 26, 2024, Dr. Bayocot highlighted Howard Garner’s Multiple Intelligence stating that every learner is unique in their own ways, reminding not to compete with each other but rather compete with oneself. “Students of Manolo Fortich, continue the thing that you do and let us always remember that the only person you should compete is the person you were yesterday,” he stressed. Bayocot also emphasized the need of grade 10 and 12 students to be fully equipped in the upcoming National Achievement Test (NAT) to reach the national average with regards to the percentage. “In the National Achievement Test conducted last year, meron po tayong paaralan na nakapasok sa top 10 performing schools, but we are also sad that many of our schools have not reached the national average in terms of percentage kaya kailangan pa rin natin na ipagpatuloy ang pag-aaral,” he said. He further gathered the school faculty and staff, motivating them to provide quality delivery in education with ambition and inspiration. “I’m always inspired by what the teachers are doing. Manolo Fortich National High School, I know that there’s so much to learn from this institution and I’d like to really thank the principal for providing leadership that our teachers and students need,” Bayocot conveyed. For him, students must take advantage of the opportunities and use it as a weapon in overcoming challenges in the future. Furthermore, the Department of Education (DepEd) Region X ensures that the department’s level of governance will do its respective monitoring and evaluation, collaborating findings for the next step.
